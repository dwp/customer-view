21 July 2023 - 31 August 2023

Iteration 3

V3   Alpha   Complete

Iterations based on user research round 13 and some mock assessment design recommendations.

We used this design as a demo for our alpha assessment on 5 July 2023, which we passed. We haven’t tested this version with users.

What's new?
  • new Welsh toggle
  • new ‘how did you find out about this service?’ beta eligibility question
  • added ‘not eligible’ signposting pages for:
    • users who haven’t been invited in beta
    • users who want to change bank details for ‘someone else’
    • users who get ‘a different benefit’ like Universal Credit only
    • users who don’t have a UK bank account
  • new eligibility results page for users who have a payment due
  • added name of benefit and last digits of bank details to personal details
  • iterated ‘do you need payments to go into one account’ content
  • iterated ‘before you change account details’ page
  • new signposting page if user decides not to change details while signed in
  • iterated ‘what happens next’ on confirmation page based on payment process and robot times
  • new ‘save or print’ link on confirmation page

2 June 23 - 29 June 2023

Iteration 2

V2   Alpha   Complete

Second design of the change bank details feature for user testing. This design is based on findings from user research round 11, and user research round 12.

This design only includes the list version of the homepage after user research shown it was more user-friendly.

What's new?
  • eligibility questions based on MVP scope and current tech,
    • including:
    • what do you need to change?
    • whose payments are you changing?
    • which benefits do you get?
    • where do your benefits get paid?
    • do your benefits get paid into a UK bank account?
    • is your new account based in the UK?
    • Do you have a payment due within the next 6 working days?
  • the homepage using the card design has now been eliminated
  • brief eligibility results page
  • list design homepage now only includes MVP features: change bank details and view personal details
  • new ‘which benefits do you need to change?’ page
  • user can change bank details for more than one benefit
  • new ‘do you need payments to go into one bank?’
  • new managing expectations ‘before you change bank details’ page
  • removed ‘enter your current or existing bank details’ page
  • simplified ‘check your answers’ page
  • slightly more context about ‘what happens next’ on confirmation page

We started iterating this design in sprint 25 and tested in sprint 33, user research round 13.

1 Feb 23 - 9 May 2023

Homepage design survey

V1.1   Alpha   Complete

We tested two homepage designs in a Citizen Me user research study, to understand which homepage is more user-friendly.

Second iteration of the card designs based on findings from user research round 11, plus the same lists homepage used in the last round of research.

What's new?
  • radios have now been eliminated, and we are only testing the card and lists design
  • cards design now has all 6 features on homepage, instead of 3, based on findings from user research round 11
  • ‘Thank you for testing’ end pages if user selects something other than what we have asked

We completed this design in sprint 26 and the survey ran in sprint 31, user research round 12. The outcomes of user research suggested the list design was more user friendly.

1 Feb 23 - 9 May 2023

Iteration 1

V1   Alpha   Complete

First iteration of the new MVP for the change of bank details feature.

We tested three homepage designs, including radios, lists and cards as a continuation of our previous work on the ‘change address’ MVP.

Technical constraints meant that we had to ask users for their ‘old’ bank details before they could provide new ones.

What's new?
  • the start page reflects the vision of the service, not the MVP yet
  • user can select just one benefit to change bank details for
  • user needs to enter existing bank details before providing new ones (tech constraint)
  • enter your new bank details screen
  • check your answers before sending page
  • basic confirmation page while waiting for business process

This design was created in Alpha sprint 23 and tested in sprint 25, user research round 11.
