Iteration 3: alpha assessment demo

V3.0   Alpha   Complete

Iterations based on user research round 13 and some mock assessment design recommendations.

We used this design as a demo for our alpha assessment on 5 July 2023, which we passed. We haven’t tested this version with users.

What's new?
  • new Welsh toggle
  • new ‘how did you find out about this service?’ beta eligibility question
  • added ‘not eligible’ signposting pages for:
  • users who haven’t been invited in beta
  • users who want to change bank details for ‘someone else’
  • users who get ‘a different benefit’ like Universal Credit only
  • users who don’t have a UK bank account
  • context about why we ask if the user has a payment due in eligibility questions
  • new eligibility results page for users who have a payment due
  • personal details now has benefit names and last digits of bank details
  • iterated ‘do you need payments to go into one account’ content
  • iterated ‘before you change account details’ page
  • new signposting page if user decides not to change details while signed in
  • iterated ‘what happens next’ on confirmation page based on payment process and robot times
  • new ‘save or print’ link on confirmation page

View the design feedback from the external alpha assessment on Mural.
