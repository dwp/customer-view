Task-based design

V1.1   Alpha   Complete

The task-based design lets users select what they want to do on the service, instead of a traditional dashboard design.

We tested and iterated this design during alpha sprint 2 - 9.

What’s in this design?
  • service start page for MVP at the time
  • menu (or homepage)
  • check my next benefit payment
  • view payment history
  • prove to someone that I’m getting benefits
  • view my personal details
  • view my bank details
  • ask questions about my benefits - not functional
  • check my contact history with benefit services - not functional
  • return to menu options on each transaction page
  • sign out

All-in-one design

V1.0   Alpha   Complete

The all-in-one design is more like a traditional homepage or dashboard.

We tested and iterated this design during alpha sprint 1 - 9. This is the last iteration of the all-in-one design.

What’s in this design?
  • service start page for MVP at the time
  • benefits summary
    • payment amount and next payment date
  • benefit details:
    • proof of benefit letter for each benefit
    • payment history for each benefit
  • navigation bar showing:
    • payment history
    • proof of benefit information and digital evidence
    • contact an advisor form with benefit contacts
    • contact history - not functional
    • basic personal details, including bank details
    • help - not functional
  • sign out button and sign out page
