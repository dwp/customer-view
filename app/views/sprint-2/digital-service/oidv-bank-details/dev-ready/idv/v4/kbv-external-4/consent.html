Confirm your identity Questions from your credit record

To confirm your identity, we need to ask you some questions about your credit record. This will not affect your credit rating.

DWP are accessing information from a 3rd party credit reference agency, because we don't have enough information about you, to prove who you are. We need to ask these questions to help protect you against fraud.

We are only using this information to prove your identity, and we will not store it in our database.

This will not affect your benefits claim, and it will not affect your credit rating.
