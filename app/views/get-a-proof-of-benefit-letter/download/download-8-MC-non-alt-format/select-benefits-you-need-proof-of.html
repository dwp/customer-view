Getting proof of your benefits

You can get proof of some of your benefits using this service, but not all of them.

This is because not all of your benefits are online yet.

Benefits you cannot get proof of using this service

To get proof of Carer's Allowance, you need to contact them.

Find their contact details in a letter they have sent to you, or in guidance about the benefit on GOV.UK (opens in a new tab).

Benefits you can get proof of using this service

If you continue, you can request proof of Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) and Personal Independence Payment (PIP).

What do you need proof of?

You can select more than one.
