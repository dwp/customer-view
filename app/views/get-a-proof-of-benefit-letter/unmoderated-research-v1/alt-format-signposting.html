How to get proof of your benefits and State Pension in another format

You need to contact the benefit team directly.

For Universal Credit, sign in to your Universal Credit account (opens in a new tab).

You can also contact the Universal Credit helpline (opens in a new tab).

For Attendance Allowance, Disability Living Allowance and Personal Independence Payment, contact the Disability Service Centre (opens in a new tab).

For Pension Credit and State Pension, contact the Pension Service (opens in a new tab).
