Get a proof of benefit letter

Use this service to request a letter to prove you get:

  • Attendance Allowance
  • Disability Living Allowance (DLA) for adults
  • any type of Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Income Support
  • Industrial Injuries Disablement Benefit
  • Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • Pension Credit
  • State Pension

You must currently get at least one of these benefits to request a letter.

When you request a letter, it can take up to one week to arrive in the post.

If you’re using this service for the first time

You will need to register if you have not used this service before.

To register, you'll need:

  • an email address
  • a mobile phone
  • your National Insurance number

We’ll ask you about 2 types of identification to help protect you from fraud.

You may be asked about:

  • a valid UK passport
  • payslips or P60
  • information about tax credits
  • details of your bank accounts, loans, mortgages or credit agreements
  • Self Assessment tax returns if you have a business or other income

I do not have any of these

If you have used this service before

You’ll need the email address, mobile phone and password you used to register.
