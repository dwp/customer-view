Change log

Since version 1, we have:

  • Reduced and iterated content on the start page
  • Removed ID requirements on the start page
  • Added signposting pages for people who are not eligible in beta
  • Kept account homepage design A
  • Removed personal details homepage design B
  • Changed ‘go back to account home’ link on personal details page to a button
  • Added ‘go back to account home’ buttons on signposting pages within the service and confirmation page
  • Added out-of-scope benefits to research set-up page to determine user’s benefits and the ‘which benefits do you need proof of?’ page
  • Named out-of-scope benefits on the in-and-out-of-scope benefits signposting page
  • Iterated the content on the in-and-out-of-scope benefits signposting page
  • Iterated content on confirmation page and named out-of-scope benefits for signposting
  • Removed address change link from check your answers page
  • Added signed out and timed out pages

We’re conducting A/B testing to see which flow works best for users.

Flow A: account homepage

Flow A has an account homepage with two links on: view your personal details, and get your proof of benefit letter.

Flow B: personal details homepage

Flow B has a homepage with personal details on, and a link to the proof of benefits feature.

Change log

This version includes:

  • A happy path for users who get in-scope benefits
  • A happy path for users whose address is correct
  • Signposting for users whose benefits are out-of-scope
  • Signposting for users whose address needs updating
  • Dynamic content depending on the benefits the user gets
  • Dynamic content depending on whether the user has a correspondence address saved on our records
  • The OIDV journey
