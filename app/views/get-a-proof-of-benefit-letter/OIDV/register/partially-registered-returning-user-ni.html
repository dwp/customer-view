You must complete your registration before you sign in

You need a mobile phone to complete your registration. We will send a security code to your mobile phone.

When you sign in for the first time

You will need information from 2 sources so we can check we are dealing with the right person. These are:

  • a UK passport
  • UK driving licence
  • payslips or P60
  • information about tax credits, if you claim them
  • details of your credit record
