Proof of your Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

The proof of PIP letter includes:

  • your name and address
  • which parts of PIP you get - there is a daily living part and a mobility part
  • whether you get the standard or enhanced rate for the parts you get
  • the date your PIP starts and ends
To get a letter that includes points from your PIP assessment, you need to contact PIP (opens in a new tab).
