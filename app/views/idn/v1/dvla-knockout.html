Do you have a UK photo driving licence?

You can confirm your identity by entering some information from a driving licence that has your photo on it. You can use either a full or provisional driving licence. If you do not have a driving licence, we will help you to confirm your identity another way.

To confirm your identity using your driving licence, we need your consent to share your information with the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency (DVLA).
