Online self-service

Online self-service lets people access their benefit information online.

People can view their benefits payments, view personal details, and get proof of their benefits when it suits them. This gives people a convenient alternative to phoning DWP and makes managing finances easier.

The focus of alpha is to design and test a framework with a diverse range of users, iterating the designs to improve how they navigate the service.

All-in-one design

The all-in-one design lets users view all their benefit information in one place.

This design shows the team’s work from alpha sprint 1 to sprint 9.

The team stopped iterating the all-in-one design during sprint 9 in favour of the task-based design.
