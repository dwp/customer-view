Customer View prototypes

Customer View lets people view and do things with their benefits and State Pension online.

We currently have designed prototypes for 4 different scopes:

  • Requesting a proof of benefit letter
  • Change bank details
  • Change your address
  • Check your payments

We also designed some prototypes as experiments during the discovery phase that included multiple of the scopes listed above.
