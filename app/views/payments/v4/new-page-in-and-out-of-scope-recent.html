Payments you can view on this service

Continue to recent payments to view your payments for:

  • Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

Payments you cannot view on this service

You cannot use this service to view your payments for:

  • Incapacity Benefit
  • Universal Credit

You will need to contact each benefit team directly.

Find their contact details in a letter they have sent to you, or in guidance about the benefit on GOV.UK (opens in a new tab).

To view your Universal Credit payments, sign in to your Universal Credit account (opens in a new tab).
