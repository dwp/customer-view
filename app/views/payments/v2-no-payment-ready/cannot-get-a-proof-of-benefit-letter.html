You cannot get proof of your benefits using this service

To get proof of your benefits, you need to contact each benefit that pays you.

How to get prood of your benefits

Find the contact details for your benefits in a letter they have sent to you, or in guidance about the benefit (opens in a new tab).

To get proof of Universal Credit, sign in to your Universal Credit account (opens in a new tab).

For State Pension, contact the Pension Service (opens in a new tab).
