Change log

This version includes:

  • Ticket 2549 - changed 'Contact us' H1 to: 'You do not have any recent payments' / 'We cannot show your next payments' - provided body text to explain 3 day/ 3 month time spans.

  • Ticket 2623 - designed new H2 and body text to cover scenarios where a payment has been rejected, recalled, returned or canceled.

  • Ticket 2622 - updated H2 and body text content to better describe when to call in or what 'something wrong' means (added missing).

  • Ticket 2626 - tweaks to H1, changes to H2 and body text to remove conflicting message between headers for handling out of scope benefits only. Introduced bullets for list of 2 out of scope benefits or over, new lead in text for bullets and out of scope across different dynamic text variations.

  • Ticket 2555.
