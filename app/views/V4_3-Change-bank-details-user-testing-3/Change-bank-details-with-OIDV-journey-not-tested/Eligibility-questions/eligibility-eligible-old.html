You can use this service

Based on your answers, you can change your account details using this service.

You will need

Your bank, building society or credit union account details, or the details of a family member or trusted friend, including the:

  • account number
  • sort code
  • roll number if you have a building society account
  • membership number if you have a credit union

Building society or credit unions

You will need the roll number if you have a building society account.

If you have a credit union account, you will need the membership number.

What happens next

To keep your information secure, we will ask you security questions and you will need to register to use the service.
